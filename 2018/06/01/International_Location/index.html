The International Location of Mozambique

Where is Mozambique?

Mozambique is a small country in the south-east part of Africa, the neighbouring countries of the nation being: Tanzania, Malawi, Zambia, Zimbabwe, Swaziland and South Africa. To the east coast of Mozambique, the Indian Ocean lies as the main supplier of seafood for Mozambicans.

General Information on Mozambique:

Capital City:

The capital city of Mozambique is Maputo, the area originally being a fishing village established in the 1500s.

Other Major Cities:

Some of the top major cities in Mozambique are: Matola, Nampula, Beira and Chimoio.

Climate in Mozambique:

Normally, the climate is warm and tropical, the year consisting of two seasons, the wet, (October-March), and the dry, (April-September). Cyclones occur frequently during the wet season.
